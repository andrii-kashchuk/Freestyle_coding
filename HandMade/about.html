About Me

Let's take a closer look. I live in Ukraine in the suburbs of the capital - the city of Kiev. I am engaged in knitting for over 40 years. I know techniques: technique 1, technique 2, technique 3. During this time I have been knitted over 500 different things, such as:

  • Socks;
  • Scarves;
  • Dresses for children;
  • Cardigans;
  • Sweaters of different knitting techniques;
  • Berets;
  • and other things.

After my family and friends began to wear things I tied, the people around them began to be interested in where you can buy or order something like that and individual requests to make this or that thing under the order. This was the beginning of my commercial activity. So, if you want to be individual and wear exclusive handmade items - welcome to our club!
