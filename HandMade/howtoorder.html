Idea

If you like exclusive things and want something not standard, rather even exclusive, then you just need to contact me and give me information on how you see such a thing in your view. Perhaps you can provide a photo or a sample of this thing that you want and I will be very happy to start making it for you!

Specify your parameters

Before we start, please provide the following information about yourself: Measuring of your bust. Measuring of your waist. Measuring of your hips and maybe some additional information by request.

Formation of prices and terms

When all the necessary information is received, we will analyze it and provide you with the cost of materials and work. We will also stipulate the approximate terms of this work.

Approval of the layout

After clarifying all the details for the order, you will be provided with a model of the future product for approval. After its approval from your side, we will begin to translate your idea into reality.

Prepayment

As soon as all of the above points are agreed, we will expect an advance payment from you. After confirmation of receipt of funds, the process of manufacturing the product begins.

Product manufacturing

Manufacturing takes a certain amount of time and depends on the complexity and volume of work. If you need to make changes to the approved layout, then we are always happy to go to a meeting with the client.

Delivery

After the product is ready, we will arrange for its shipment to the address indicated to you, if you are located outside of Kiev, Ukraine. The choice of a mailing counterparty is at your discretion.

Feedback

We look forward to hearing from you about the item you ordered. Your opinion is very important to us and the main thing is that you are 100% satisfied! From our side, we will do our best to make this happen.
